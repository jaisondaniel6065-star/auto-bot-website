Warranty Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Coverage

All products and installation services performed by Autobot Automotive are covered by our warranty. Coverage periods vary by product category:

Electronics (Android Screens, Cameras, Audio)

12 months on parts, 6 months on installation labour

Leather & Interior Upholstery

6 months on stitching and fitment

Ceramic Coating & PPF

As per manufacturer warranty (typically 2–5 years)

Floor Mats & Accessories

3 months on manufacturing defects

What Is Not Covered

  • Damage caused by accidents, misuse, or negligence
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by third-party modifications after our service
  • Water damage not resulting from our installation

How to Claim

To make a warranty claim, contact us with your service receipt and a description of the issue. Our team will assess the claim and schedule a rectification appointment at no charge if the issue falls within warranty coverage.
